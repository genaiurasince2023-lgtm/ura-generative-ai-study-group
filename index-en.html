About Our Study Group

The "Study Group for Generative AI in URA Operations" is a community dedicated to exploring how generative AI can be utilized in the work of University Research Administrators (URAs). Our goal is to learn about the latest AI technology trends and share practical ideas and case studies for improving operational efficiency.

October 15, 2023

History

Our study group was founded in the summer of 2023 by a few volunteers. Initially a small gathering, it has grown with the increasing interest in generative AI and has now become a community with participants from many universities and research institutions.
